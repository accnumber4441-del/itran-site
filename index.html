features

designed around you

Every feature serves a purpose — fewer clicks, calmer browsing, more control.

Vertical Tabs

Tree-style sidebar tabs that give you back horizontal space. Collapse, nest, and organize effortlessly.

Privacy First

Built on Firefox with telemetry stripped out. No tracking, no data collection — just browsing.

Deep Customization

CSS theming, custom mods, and full control over every UI element. Make the browser truly yours.

Workspaces

Separate contexts for work, personal, and projects — each with its own tabs, containers, and theme.

Split View

Open two pages side by side without separate windows. Perfect for comparisons and research.

Compact UI

Minimal chrome with auto-hiding toolbars. Maximum screen space for what matters: the content.
